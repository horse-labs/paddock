# Support

Need help with Paddock? Here's where to go.

- **Docs first** — [`README.md`](README.md), [`FRAMEWORK.md`](FRAMEWORK.md), and [`docs/`](docs/)
  (start with [`docs/getting-started.md`](docs/getting-started.md) and [`docs/workspace-setup.md`](docs/workspace-setup.md)).
- **Landing / overview** — https://horse-labs.github.io/paddock/
- **Questions & ideas** — open a **[Discussion](https://github.com/horse-labs/paddock/discussions)** (Q&A or Ideas).
- **Bugs & feature requests** — open an **[Issue](https://github.com/horse-labs/paddock/issues/new/choose)**
  (use the templates).
- **Security** — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) (do not use public issues).

Paddock is a small project maintained by [HorseLabs](https://github.com/horse-labs) — please be patient and kind.
